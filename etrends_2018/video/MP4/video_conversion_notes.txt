To change air pollution video from 16x9 to 4x3:

1. Go to https://www.online-convert.com and convert mp4 to avi setting kbps to 400 and codec to h264
2. Open VirtualDub on desktop and create a Video-->Filter-->resize to 4:3
3. Select Video-->Compression and use the h264 codec to enable compression of saved avi
4. Save as .avi
5. Go back to https://www.online-convert.com and convert to mp4, disable audio track and h264 codec
6. Go to http://www.videosmaller.com/ and compress final 4x3 mp4 video
7. Create snapshot using VSDC
